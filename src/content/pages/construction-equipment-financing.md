## Equipment commonly financed
- Skid steers
- Excavators
- Dump trucks
- Loaders
- Compact track loaders
- Trailers

## Why this product fits contractors
Equipment generates revenue. Financing can help match payments to the cash flow those machines help create.

## When to use equipment financing
Use it when you need machinery to take on jobs, replace failing equipment, or avoid draining cash reserves.
