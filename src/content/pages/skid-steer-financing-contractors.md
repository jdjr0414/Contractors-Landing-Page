## How do contractors finance skid steers?

Contractors typically use [construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing) to purchase skid steers and compact track loaders. Payments are structured to match the revenue the equipment generates. The equipment serves as collateral. Both new and used units may qualify. For general equipment financing, see [construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing). For operating needs, see [contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital). ## When do contractors typically need skid steer financing?

Contractors need it when **taking on new or larger jobs** that require a skid steer. **Replacing failing equipment** when repair no longer makes sense. **Expanding the fleet** to handle more projects. **Upgrading** to compact track loaders or newer models.
