## When contractors use lines of credit
A line of credit is often used for recurring expenses like payroll float, supplier timing, or small equipment needs.

## Good fit scenarios
- Multiple overlapping jobs
- Timing gaps in receivables
- Seasonal slowdowns
- Small recurring expenses

## Line of credit vs working capital
A line of credit is often revolving and flexible. Working capital is often simpler for a one-time immediate need.
