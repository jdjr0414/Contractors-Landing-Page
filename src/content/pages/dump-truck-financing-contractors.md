## How do contractors finance dump trucks?

Contractors typically use [construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing) to purchase dump trucks. Payments are structured to match the revenue the vehicle generates. The truck often serves as collateral. Both new and used trucks may qualify. For general equipment financing, see [construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing). For operating needs, see [contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital). For flexible access, see [contractor line of credit](/contractor-line-of-credit). ## When do contractors typically need dump truck financing?

Contractors need it when **taking on new or larger jobs** that require a dump truck. **Replacing failing equipment** when repair no longer makes sense. **Expanding the fleet** to handle more projects. **Upgrading** to newer or larger capacity.
