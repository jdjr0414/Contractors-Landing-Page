## Why contractors use working capital
Contractors often pay for labor and materials before customer funds arrive. Working capital helps smooth those gaps.

## Common use cases
- Payroll between draws
- Material purchases
- Mobilization costs
- Emergency repairs
- Marketing or expansion

## What lenders often look at
Revenue history, time in business, bank activity, average deposits, and the reason for funds.

## Contractor working capital vs line of credit
Working capital can be faster and simpler. A line of credit may offer more flexibility over time. The right fit depends on urgency and qualification.
