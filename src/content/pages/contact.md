## About this site

Contractor Capital Guide provides educational information for contractors exploring financing options. We cover topics such as working capital, equipment financing, lines of credit, payroll funding, and cash flow management.

## For content questions

If you have questions about our guides, blog posts, or how we present information, you can reach out through the contact methods listed on this page. We aim to keep our content accurate and helpful.

## For funding inquiries

This site does not provide funding directly. If you are looking for contractor financing options, we may link to external resources. Those links are disclosed as sponsored where applicable. For specific funding products, please contact the relevant provider.
