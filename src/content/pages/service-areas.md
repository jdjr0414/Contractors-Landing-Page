## Nationwide contractor funding

Contractor financing options are available to construction businesses in **all 50 states**. Whether you operate in Texas, California, Florida, New York, or any other state, you can access [contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital), [construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing), [contractor lines of credit](/contractor-line-of-credit), and [construction business loans](/construction-business-loans). There are no geographic restrictions for these products. Lenders serve contractors nationwide.

## What funding options are available nationwide?

- **[Contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital)** – Payroll, materials, and operating gaps
- **[Construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing)** – Excavators, skid steers, dump trucks, machinery
- **[Contractor line of credit](/contractor-line-of-credit)** – Revolving access for recurring needs
- **[Contractor payroll funding](/contractor-payroll-funding)** – Bridge payroll when invoices are delayed
- **[Construction business loans](/construction-business-loans)** – Expansion, acquisition, larger projects
- **[Accounts receivable financing](/accounts-receivable-financing-contractors)** – Convert invoices to cash

## No geographic limits

Contractor funding is not limited by city, county, or region. Construction businesses in rural areas, suburbs, and major metros can qualify. The key factors are typically revenue, time in business, and the specific need—not your location. For more on how these options work, see [contractor cash flow problems](/contractor-cash-flow-problems) and the [contractor cash flow guide](/contractor-cash-flow-guide).
