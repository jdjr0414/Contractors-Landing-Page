## Can contractors get financing with bad credit?

Some products and lenders focus on **revenue**, **time in business**, and **bank activity** rather than credit scores. [Construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing) may use the equipment as collateral—the lender's recovery is the asset. [Accounts receivable financing](/accounts-receivable-financing-contractors) may rely on client creditworthiness. Some [contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital) products use different criteria than traditional bank loans. Terms and rates may vary. For a full overview, see [contractor cash flow problems](/contractor-cash-flow-problems) and [all funding options](/all-funding-options).

## What options may be easier with credit challenges?

**Equipment financing** — The equipment secures the loan. Lenders may weigh the asset and revenue more than personal credit. See [construction equipment financing](/construction-equipment-financing), [excavator financing](/excavator-financing-contractors), and [used construction equipment financing](/used-construction-equipment-financing).

**Accounts receivable financing** — Invoice financing may rely on client credit. If your clients pay, the factor may advance funds. See [accounts receivable financing for contractors](/accounts-receivable-financing-contractors).

**Working capital** — Some products emphasize revenue and bank activity. See [contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital). For preparation, see [how to prepare for contractor financing approval](/blog/prepare-contractor-financing-approval/).

## How to improve approval chances

Clean financials, strong revenue history, and applying when not in crisis can help. Document your revenue, bank activity, and the reason for funds. Some products may be easier to qualify for than traditional bank loans. Applying before you need the funds—when you have time to gather documentation—can improve outcomes. For a full preparation guide, see [how to prepare for contractor financing approval](/blog/prepare-contractor-financing-approval/).
