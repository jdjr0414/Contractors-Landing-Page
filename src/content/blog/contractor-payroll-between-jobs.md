Contractor payroll problems usually come from timing. Labor has to be paid every week or two, but many jobs do not pay on the same schedule.

## Why the timing problem happens
Construction companies often front labor before a milestone payment or invoice is collected.

## Common solutions contractors consider
- speeding up collections
- negotiating payment timing
- using working capital
- using a line of credit

## When funding can help
Funding can help when jobs are profitable but cash arrives too slowly to support payroll.
