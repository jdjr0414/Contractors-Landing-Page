Multiple projects create overlapping cash flows. This guide explains [contractor cash flow problems](/contractor-cash-flow-problems) and how [contractor line of credit](/contractor-line-of-credit) and [contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital) help.

## What funding options help with project cash flow?

A [contractor line of credit](/contractor-line-of-credit) is commonly used for overlapping projects. [Contractor working capital](/contractor-working-capital) can bridge specific gaps. For payroll, see [contractor payroll funding](/contractor-payroll-funding). For materials, see [contractor material purchase financing](/contractor-material-purchase-financing).
