  - question: How can contractors improve approval odds?
    answer: Having clean financials, understanding your needs, and applying when you are not in crisis can help. Some products may be easier to qualify for than traditional bank loans.
  - question: When should contractors apply for financing?
    answer: Before you need it, when possible. Having a line of credit in place before the slow season or overlapping jobs can provide flexibility. Applying when already short can be harder.

    answer: Bank statements, revenue documentation, and information about the business. Equipment financing may require equipment details. Requirements vary by product and lender.
